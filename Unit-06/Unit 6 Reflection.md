# Unit 6 Reflection

In Unit 6, I learned about testing, iteration, links, and gathering client feedback. Testing is an iterative process where designs are improved based on feedback. I asked classmates to review my page and learned to listen without defending my choices.

I learned to add hyperlinks using the <a> tag. Absolute links use a full URL to go to another website. I used target="_blank" to open links in a new tab. With CSS, I styled links using pseudo-classes like :hover and :visited to change colors and remove underlines.

I also polished my page by adding a favicon, which is a small icon in the browser tab. I styled images using border, border-radius, box-shadow, and margin to make them look more professional.

Finally, I gathered feedback from my client Sara. She loved the colors but wanted more personal stories about her grandmother. I added a family story section, a real family photo, and better recipe descriptions. Getting direct client feedback was exciting and valuable because only the client knows what truly feels like her.
